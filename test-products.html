TESTED PRODUCTS

Our laboratories analyse and certify items such as footwear, gloves, clothing, and impact protectors to ensure the products compliance with applicable standards demanded by the market.

We certify protective equipment for firefighters, the high visibility sector and the motorcycle world. RICOTEST is the ideal technological partner for testing the quality of products designed for mountain and outdoor activities as well as products of the textile, clothing and leather industry.

FOOTWEAR

Since 1982 RICOTEST is the first italian Certification Institute specialised in footwear industry.

PROTECTORS

We are also specialised in testing impact protection devices for various sports.

AIRBAGS

Our laboratories test motorcycle airbags and in general inflatable protectors.

MOTORCYCLING

RICOTEST has achieved the outmost competence in testing the quality of items for the motorcycling industry.

FIREFIGHTING

Our laboratories certify the quality of clothing, footwear and protection gloves for Firefighters.

MATERIALS

We do not only analyse finished products but also loose materials such as leather, textiles and plastics.
